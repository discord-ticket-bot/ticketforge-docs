## Support Tier

We also offer a **Support** tier. This includes everything in Premium, plus:

- **Roadmap Access:** Vote on upcoming features.
- **Supporter Role:** Special badge in our support server.
- **Priority Support:** Faster response times from our team.

## How to Upgrade

1.  Go to the **Premium** tab in the dashboard.
2.  Select **Monthly** or **Yearly** (20% discount).
3.  Click **Get Started** on the plan you want.
4.  Complete the secure checkout via Stripe.
5.  Your server will be instantly upgraded.
